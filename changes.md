**Changes made from curent Settings Panel to new one**

### Major Layout & Structural Changes:
- **New "Authorized plates" panel** added in the top-right corner. 
  - This is the most significant addition. The long horizontal text field at the bottom of Snapshot 1 (`8255ZM30|CF938PH|CV424MM|DJ563QK`) has been removed.
  - Its content has been moved into this new clean, dedicated box that displays each license plate on its own line.

- **Reorganized lower sections**: 
  - **Relay** and **Polling** sections, which were stacked vertically in Snapshot 1, are now placed **side-by-side** in Snapshot 2. This creates a more balanced, two-column layout.

- **Overall grid layout improvement**:
  - The interface now uses a more modern card-based, two-column structure in the upper half (Camera on left, Authorized plates on right).
  - The **Region of Interest** section remains visually similar but now sits as a full-width section below the top two panels.

### Minor/Visual Changes:
- The **Camera** section remains in the same position and contains identical values.
- The **Region of Interest** preview, orange selection box, coordinates, timestamp, and "Reset ROI" button are unchanged.
- All values in **Relay** and **Polling** sections are identical.
- "Save & Apply" and "Cancel" buttons at the bottom are unchanged.
- Slight adjustments to spacing, padding, and panel borders to accommodate the new layout.

### Summary:
The update transforms the GUI from a mostly vertical, stacked layout into a more organized, horizontally balanced design. The biggest functional improvement is turning the hard-to-read pipe-separated license plate string into a clean, dedicated **Authorized plates** list. The changes make the interface feel less cramped and more professional.
